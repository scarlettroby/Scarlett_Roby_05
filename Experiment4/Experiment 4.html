⏳ Time Dilation: A Journey Through Time

Ever wondered how time behaves differently for astronauts traveling at high speeds or near massive objects? Let's explore the fascinating concept of time dilation!

Interesting Facts About Time Dilation

  • Time moves slower for objects moving close to the speed of light compared to those at rest (Special Relativity).
  • Gravity can also slow down time — clocks near massive objects run slower (General Relativity).
  • Astronauts on long space missions experience time slightly differently than people on Earth.
  • GPS satellites have to account for time dilation to provide accurate positioning.
